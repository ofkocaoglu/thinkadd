LPBF Material Universe

Laser Powder Bed Fusion (LPBF) is a metal additive manufacturing process that uses a laser to melt thin layers of metal powder, building parts layer by layer. This site is a neutral, non-commercial resource that lists both commercial and research-stage LPBF powders, summarizing their compositions, properties, and typical applications, independent of any supplier or machine brand.

Commercially available materials are highlighted in blue; hover list items to see composition.
